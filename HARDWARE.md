# 🔌 Circuit Wiring

## 📍 Connections

| Component | Raspberry Pi 4 Pin | GPIO |
|-----------|--------------------|------|
| LED Anode (+) | Pin 12 | GPIO18 |
| LED Cathode (-) | 220Ω Resistor | — |
| Resistor | Pin 6 | GND |

---

## ⚠️ Resistor Requirement

A **220Ω resistor** is used to limit the current flowing through the LED.  
This protects both the **LED** and the **Raspberry Pi GPIO pin** from damage.
